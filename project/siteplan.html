Website Planning Document

Site Name

The Cinephile's Corner

This name represents a personal selection of movies, implying a wide scope of cinematic choices viewed through a personal lens.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a personal portfolio showcasing my favorite movies. It aims to provide basic information about each film and explain why I appreciate them, offering a glimpse into my cinematic taste.

Scenarios

  • What are some movies that the site owner recommends, and why?
  • What kind of cinematic elements (acting, cinematography, soundtrack) does the site owner focus on when analyzing a film?

Color Schema

  • Red (#e50914): Used for headings and accents, representing the drama and passion of cinema.
  • White (#fff): Used for the background, providing a clean and focused viewing experience.
  • Dark Gray (#333): Used for paragraph text, for readability.

Typography

  • Headings: Georgia, serif
  • Body: Arial, sans-serif
